Privacy Policy

Szczepanikov Analytics — private, read‑only tool

Data sources. The app uses official APIs (TikTok/Instagram) with read‑only scopes to fetch the owner’s content metrics and comments.

Data collected. Video/reel IDs, titles, timestamps, public metrics (views, likes, comments), and top‑liked comments. No sensitive personal data is collected.

Storage. Metrics are processed transiently to produce weekly reports. Tokens are stored locally and can be revoked at any time.

Sharing. No data is sold or shared with third parties.

User rights. Not applicable — the app has no external users and only processes the owner's data.
